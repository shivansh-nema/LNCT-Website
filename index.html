About LNCT Group

Established in 1994, LNCT Group has been at the forefront of technical education in Central India

Our Legacy

LNCT Group of Colleges stands as a beacon of excellence in technical education, established with the vision of creating world-class engineers and technologists. Over the past 25+ years, we have consistently maintained our position as one of the premier educational institutions in Madhya Pradesh.

Our commitment to quality education, industry-relevant curriculum, and holistic development has produced thousands of successful professionals who are making significant contributions across various sectors globally.

NAAC A+ Accredited
Multiple Campuses

Our Mission

To provide world-class technical education and foster innovation, research, and entrepreneurship among students while maintaining the highest standards of academic excellence.

Our Vision

To be recognized globally as a premier institution that transforms students into competent professionals and responsible citizens who contribute to society's progress.
